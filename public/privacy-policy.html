Privacy Policy

At Satta King Fast Results, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our website and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other websites, we use log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement around the site, and gather broad demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We do use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access, and customize our web page content based on visitors browser type.

Third Party Privacy Policies

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Our privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or websites.

Children's Information

We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to participate in their online activities and monitor their use. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
